Keep Yourself Out of the Circuit.

Keeping yourself out of an electical circuit requires diligence and knowledge. You will need to learn the basics of how electricity flows through a circuit, what a circuit is, and constant awareness of your sourandings.

Electrical shocks, arc flash explosions, and electocutions can be avoided and you are the key to make this happen.

Electrical shocks happen when you become part of an electrical circuit and the current flows through your body. Electocution is when the current is high emough to cause death by disrupting the heart or other vital organs. Your path in the conductor and the current level is high enough to keep you as part of the circuit. Arch flash incidences are when a current flows from the ciruit to a short circuit to ground. Usually a high energy and high temperature explosion occurs and if you are in the blast zone, severe injury or death can be the result.
